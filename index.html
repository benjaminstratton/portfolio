About Me

Over the past two years, my skills have been looked upon to help start-up companies create acquisition channels and manage sales ecosystems. Through this experience, I was able to develop a deep understanding and skill set in sales, marketing, and business that I used to close over half a million in sales within my first year as a sales representative and average a 500% increase in sales through acquisition, marketing, and management as a business consultant. With great pleasure, I took the advice of my supervisors to continue learning and building my skill set, which eventually let me down the road of creation as a software engineer.

I am looking to launch a joyful and successful career as a developer with an innovative team to solve the intricacies and inefficiencies between business and consumer. I believe that by mixing my previous experience, love for learning, and new skillset as a developer, I will be able to accomplish this and more!
